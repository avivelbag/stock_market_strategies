# Donchian Turtle Breakout — visualised

An interactive backtest of the supply-vacuum breakout strategy in
`strategies/03-donchian-turtle-breakout/` — the original Turtle System 1.

## What it teaches

A close above the prior 20-bar high means every willing seller in that window
has been absorbed: a supply vacuum that, in trending regimes, tends to keep
lifting price. The exit is the prior 10-bar low. Breakout strategies are
explicitly regime-dependent — this page makes that dependence visible.

The price chart draws both Donchian boundaries (the entry high and the exit
low) as overlays, so you can watch price punch through the channel and see
exactly which bar fired the trade.

## What you see

1. **Price, signal & trades** — close with the 20-bar-high and 10-bar-low
   channels overlaid, held-long shading, ▲/▼ entry/exit markers.
2. **Strategy equity vs buy & hold** — net-of-cost, both from ×1.00.
3. **Metrics + interpretation** — in-sample vs walk-forward, plus the honest
   computed read on regime fit.

Compare regimes: thin in-sample margins on `trend_gbm`/`mean_rev_ou`, a
genuine edge on the trending blocks of `regime_switch`, and a walk-forward
warning on `fat_tail`.
